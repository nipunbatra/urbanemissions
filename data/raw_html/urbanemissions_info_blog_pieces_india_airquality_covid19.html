Air Quality in India During COVID-19

Date: 2020-May-08; Update: 2020-May-25; Update: 2020-Jun-03

All the data is from the official monitoring network operated and maintained by the Central Pollution Control Board, for every city with at least one air monitoring station. Data was accessed via openaq.org using their API services. All the data was cleaned and processed for quality.

  • Lockdown Phase 1: March 24th to April 14th, 2020.
  • Lockdown Phase 2: April 15th to May 3rd, 2020.
  • Lockdown Phase 3: May 4th to May 17th, 2020.
  • Lockdown Phase 4: May 18th to May 31st, 2020.
  • O3 and CO averages are for day-time only.
  • PM10, PM2.5, SO2, and NO2 averages are all-time.
